Vivian Maier

Created with Adobe Indesign.

Vivian Maier was born February 1, 1926 and died April 21, 2009. She was an American street photographer. Maier worked as a nanny while pursuing photography during her spare time. She took more than 150,000 photographs during her lifetime, primarily of the people and architecture of New York City, Chicago, and Los Angeles, although she also traveled and photographed worldwide.
